Scrambled Tofu

Ingredients:

  • handful chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1/2 block medium-firm tofu
  • salt to taste
  • salsa

Instructions:

  1. Dice the mushrooms and bell peppers into small pieces and fry over medium heat in a non-stick pan with a bit of oil (optional) Sprinkle with salt. Cook until mushrooms are browned (I like to let the mushrooms sit for a bit to brown).
  2. Add the medium-firm tofu (crumble it with fingers) and mix with the vegetables. Let it sit for a couple minutes until the tofu browns then mix well with the vegetables.
  3. Add as much salsa as you want for flavour, add salt to taste, and you are ready to eat!
